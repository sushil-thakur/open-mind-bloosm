Are You Ready For a Relationship? | Fun & Free Quiz

Entering a new relationship is a big step that can add to your joy and commitment. At the same time, you need to have emotional maturity, self-awareness, and stability to thrive in a new relationship...

Are You Ready For a Relationship?

Please answer the following questions as truthfully as you can.

1. How do you feel about your current status?

2. How well do you know yourself and your needs?

3. Are you good at handling conflicts?

4. How stable would you say your life is currently?

5. How comfortable are you with being vulnerable with another person?

6. How do you handle past relationship experiences?

7. What do you think about commitment?

8. What do you think about sharing your space and time with someone?

9. What are your thoughts about compromise in a relationship?

10. How much time do you think you can invest in a relationship?
